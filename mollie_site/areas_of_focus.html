Areas of Focus

Anxiety

Is anxiety interfering with your daily life, causing distress, and hindering your overall well-being? We understand the profound impact that anxiety can have on your mental and emotional health. Our therapy services provide a compassionate and personalized approach to help you manage anxiety, develop coping strategies, and find a sense of calm amidst the chaos.

Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety can manifest in various ways, from persistent worry and racing thoughts to physical symptoms like rapid heartbeat and shortness of breath. It can leave you feeling trapped, limiting your ability to engage in activities you once enjoyed and impacting your relationships and overall well-being.

Reclaim Control and Inner Calm

Therapy offers a safe and supportive space where you can delve into the root causes of your anxiety, challenge negative thought patterns, and develop effective coping strategies.

Disordered Eating

Do you find yourself trapped in a cycle of disordered eating patterns, feeling guilt or shame around food, and struggling with body image concerns? We specialize in helping individuals overcome disordered eating behaviors and cultivate a healthy relationship with both food and movement. Our therapy services provide a compassionate and non-judgmental space where we'll explore the underlying factors contributing to disordered eating, develop healthier coping mechanisms, and support you in fostering a positive and nourishing connection with your body and food.

Understanding Disordered Eating

Disordered eating can manifest in various ways, from engaging in restrictive eating or experiencing binge-eating episodes to obsessively preoccupying oneself with calories, weight, or body image. It can lead to an unhealthy relationship with food and may also involve an unhealthy relationship with exercise, using it as a means to control weight, shape, or compensate for food. However, healing and transformation are possible.

Healing Your Relationship with Food and Movement

We believe in the power of intuitive eating and a holistic approach to well-being in order to heal your relationship with food and movement.

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder

Are you struggling with intrusive thoughts, repetitive behaviors, or rituals that disrupt your daily life? We specialize in helping individuals overcome Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD) through evidence-based treatment approaches. We understand the challenges that OCD can bring, and our therapy services provide a compassionate and supportive space where we'll utilize Exposure and Response Prevention (EXRP), the gold standard treatment for OCD, to help you regain control, reduce anxiety, and reclaim your life.

Understanding OCD

OCD can significantly impact your daily life and well-being, trapping you in a cycle of distressing thoughts and compulsive behaviors. It may leave you feeling overwhelmed, anxious, and disconnected from the life you want to live. However, it is possible to break free from its grip and find relief.

Exposure and Response Prevention (EXRP): The Path to Freedom

We believe in empowering individuals to overcome OCD and regain control of their lives. We utilize Exposure and Response Prevention (EXRP), the most widely recognized and effective treatment for OCD.
