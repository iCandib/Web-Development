Therapy For Anxiety

My practice is dedicated to helping young adult women overcome anxiety and find inner strength. Life is a journey filled with its share of challenges, and anxiety can often be a formidable roadblock to living life to the fullest. As a seasoned Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW), I'm here to help you take back your life from the hold of anxiety, paving the way for a life characterized by confidence, tranquility, and fulfillment.

How I Can Help

Anxiety is a complex adversary that can infiltrate various facets of your life, affecting your self-esteem, relationships, work, and overall well-being. Through the implementation of well-established therapeutic techniques, I aim to provide you with the guidance and support required not just to manage but conquer anxiety. My online therapy sessions are accessible to women residing in New York and Florida. Whether your anxiety manifests as social anxiety, generalized anxiety, or specific phobias, our collaboration will set you on the path toward healing.

The Value of Online Therapy

Online therapy stands as a convenient and highly effective avenue for tackling your anxiety head-on. The flexibility to connect with me through phone or video sessions empowers you to access professional support without the need for in-person visits. It's an accessible solution that seamlessly integrates into your hectic life, allowing you to prioritize your mental health without hassle.

Ready to embark on a transformative journey towards a life with less anxiety? Don't hesitate to book a consultation and take your initial step towards a more empowered future.

Other Therapy Services with Mollie Spiesman, LCSW

I provide a comprehensive range of services designed to offer you complete support to facilitate personal growth and empower you to make meaningful changes in your life. Services include Individual Therapy, Therapy for Anxiety, Therapy for College and Graduate Students, Therapy for Young Professionals, Therapy for Women, Online Therapy, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia, Therapy for OCD, Stress Management, and more.
