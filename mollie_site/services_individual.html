Individual Therapy

I offer individual therapy for young and emerging adults, specifically women in their twenties and thirties who are struggling with a variety of concerns such as perfectionism, anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, body image concerns, disordered eating, relationship issues, OCD, transitions, and school/work stress. Our therapy sessions provide a safe and non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Through a combination of evidence-based techniques and a collaborative approach, we will work together to identify and address the underlying issues that may be holding you back from achieving your goals and living a fulfilling life.

My approach is tailored to your unique needs and goals, with a focus on helping you develop skills and strategies to manage your symptoms and improve your overall well-being. Our sessions will help you gain clarity, build resilience, and achieve greater balance in your life. This is a compassionate and non-judgmental environment where you can feel comfortable expressing your concerns and working towards meaningful change.

Virtual Therapy

Embracing Convenience and Accessibility

I recognize that your time and flexibility are valuable. That's why I offer virtual therapy sessions conducted through a secure video platform and an online client portal. With just a click, you can engage in therapy sessions from the comfort of your own home, your office, or even while traveling. Our virtual therapy platform allows you the convenience of scheduling sessions during lunch breaks, between classes, or at times that suit your busy schedule.

Embracing Convenient and Effective Mental Health Support

I specialize in providing virtual/remote therapy services for young adults, including college students, graduate students, and young professionals. I understand the unique challenges that arise during this transformative phase of life, and providing virtual therapy allows us to support you wherever you may be on your journey.

The Benefits of Virtual Therapy

  1. Flexibility and Convenience

    Virtual therapy eliminates the need for commuting or waiting rooms, providing you with the freedom to engage in therapy from any location with internet access. It allows you to seamlessly integrate therapy into your daily routine without disrupting your commitments and responsibilities.

  2. Increased Accessibility

    No matter where you are geographically located, virtual therapy ensures that distance is not a barrier to receiving quality mental health support. You can access our services regardless of your proximity to our physical location, expanding your options and opportunities for finding the right therapist for your needs.

  3. Enhanced Comfort and Privacy

    Engaging in therapy from a space where you feel most comfortable can enhance the therapeutic experience. With virtual therapy, you have the privacy and freedom to open up and explore your thoughts and emotions without the potential discomfort of a traditional therapy setting.

Our Approach: Empathy and Expertise

I prioritize providing a warm and approachable environment for clients. I understand that seeking therapy can be a vulnerable experience, and I am here to support you with empathy, compassion, and expertise. Our virtual therapy sessions are conducted by Mollie Spiesman, LCSW, a licensed clinical social worker with experience in helping young adults navigate the challenges of college life, graduate school, and the transition to the professional world.

Getting Started with Virtual Therapy

Embarking on your virtual therapy journey is simple. Just reach out to us to schedule an initial consultation. During this session, we will discuss your specific needs and goals, as well as answer any questions you may have about our virtual therapy approach. Following the consultation, we will work together to create a personalized treatment plan that aligns with your unique circumstances and aspirations.

Don't let distance, busy schedules, or other barriers hinder your access to the support and guidance you deserve. Take the first step towards improved mental health and well-being by embracing the convenience and effectiveness of virtual therapy with Mollie Spiesman LCSW PLLC.

Note: Virtual therapy services are available to individuals located in New York. Please check our website for any updates regarding service availability.

Disclaimer: Virtual therapy has proven to be an effective alternative to in-person therapy for many individuals. However, it may not be suitable for everyone. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis or emergency, please contact emergency services or your local mental health helpline immediately.
