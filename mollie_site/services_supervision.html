Clinical Supervision

Whether you are pursuing clinical licensure or simply want a space to continue your growth as a clinician, I provide off-site clinical supervision to social workers and mental health counselors in New York. I am here to provide you with the guidance, support, and training you need to succeed in your career.

Our supervision sessions are tailored to meet your unique needs and goals, and are designed to help you develop your clinical skills, deepen your understanding of theory and research, and enhance your overall professional confidence and competence. Supervision is a powerful tool for personal and professional growth, and I am honored to support you on your journey.
